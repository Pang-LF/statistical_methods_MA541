Part 3: What distribution does your data follow¶

Suppose Close_ETF follows uniform distribution.

In [125]:
print(stats.kstest(df.oil, 'uniform'))
stats.probplot(df.Close_ETF, dist='uniform', plot=plt)
plt.show()

KstestResult(statistic=0.9373134433134432, pvalue=0.0)

Although from Part 2, Close_ETF colum looks somehow follow uniform distribution. But through the qq plot, we can see this is not the case. Also, the pvalue of Kolmogorov-Smirnov test is less than 0.05, which also shows that these data soesn't follow uniform distribution.
